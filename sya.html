May 21, 2010
Challengers Enjoy Big Night at Cub Run!
Between rainstorms last Friday, the CYA/SYA Challenger baseball game that night turned out to be a great event, and fun for all involved.  The Challenger program, which provides disabled children with an opportunity to play the game of baseball,  squared off against Little Leaguers from the regular SYA baseball program for a special night of shared community, team spirit, and sportsmanship.  The large contingent of fans was able to forget about winning and losing for one great evening with the kids, despite the weather.   To read more about the game, click here.  For more information about the Challenger program...  Read More
